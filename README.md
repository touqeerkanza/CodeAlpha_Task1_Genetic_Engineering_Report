# CodeAlpha Biotechnology Internship — Task 1: Research Report

## 🧬 Recent Advancements in Biotechnology: Genetic Engineering, CRISPR, and Gene Therapy

A research report prepared for **Task 1** of the CodeAlpha Biotechnology Virtual Internship.

## 📄 About

This 1,500–2,000 word report surveys the current state of genetic engineering, with a focus on CRISPR-based gene editing and gene therapy. It covers:

- The evolution of gene-editing tools — CRISPR-Cas9, base editors, prime editors, and epigenetic editors
- CASGEVY, the first approved CRISPR-based therapy, and its regulatory momentum
- Recent (2024–2026) clinical trial advances in cardiovascular disease, oncology, and rare genetic disorders
- CRISPR-based diagnostics (Cas12a)
- Applications in agriculture and industrial biotechnology
- Ethical, safety, and regulatory considerations, including access and germline-editing debates
- Current challenges and the outlook for the field through 2026 and beyond
